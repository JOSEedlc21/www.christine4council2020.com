PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: August 20, 2020

Christine David for Atherton Town Council 2020 (“CDATC”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) has made the following Privacy Policy (“the Policy”) to provide you with information concerning how we collect and handle your personal data. This Privacy Policy applies to all products and services operated by Christine David for Atherton Town Council 2020, which include but not limited to websites, mobile websites, web applications, and mobile applications.

Please give it a thorough read to learn what data we collect, how we use this data, and what measures we have implemented to protect your privacy. Please be advised that we reserve the right to modify the Policy as we believe necessary. All changes will take effect immediately as they become available on this page. Please check this Privacy Policy regularly for updates. We may notify you of any changes if we have your contact information.

1. Our Commitment to Protect Your Data

Christine David for Atherton Town Council 2020 is committed to protecting your data. We understand your concerns over sensitive data, and we hereby reassure you that we will protect your data from misuse. We use the data for our campaign purposes only, and we will not share this data with any third parties.

2. Types of Data We Collect

(a) Information about you

Some sections of our website require you to submit information about you, which may include your first and last name, email address, and preferences. We use this information to set up campaign activities and inform you about the latest news from our campaign.

(b) Your payment information

Our payment system will collect your first and last name, email address, and the amounts you choose to donate to us. We wish to know every supporter to provide more information about how we use the funds you provide us.

3. How We Collect Data

3.1 We collect your data directly from you primarily through the products and services we operate. You will fill out the digital forms to supply the data.

3.2 If you supply data of other individuals, you should notify them of your decision in advance and let them know where to access this Privacy Policy.

4. Why We Collect Data

To Build A Better Community with All Members

Building a better Atherton community is Christine David for Atherton Town Council 2020’s commitment. We value all members of our community, and without your inputs, we cannot successfully complete this mission. Hence, we want to know your thoughts and what you expect from us.

5. Information Security

Christine David for Atherton Town Council 2020 is committed to securing your data. We store the data you supply in a secure database that we design and administrate. We have implemented cutting-edge security protocols to reduce risks that undermine information security with our full commitment.

We comply with our security policies. It is also your responsibility to keep the information we send you confidential.

6. Know Your Rights

You have the right to request for removing your data from our database, modifying the data you supplied to us, and contacting us to learn how your data has been used.

7. Links to Other Services

Our products and services may include links to other services that are not owned or operated by us. We urge you to exercise caution before supplying data to those services. You should read the privacy policies of the services you use to make your own decisions.
